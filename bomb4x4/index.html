The rules of the game:

  • Click on a square to reveal its contents.
  • If the square contains a 💎, you score 1 point.
  • If the square contains a ♥️, you score 2 point.
  • If the square contains a ⭐, you score 1 point.
  • If the square contains a 💣, your scores are gone.
  • If the square contains a 💩, you lose 2 points.
  • If the square contains a 🔄, the teams swap scores.
