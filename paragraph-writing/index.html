Writing a Good Body Paragraph

  • Start the paragraph with a strong topic sentence that presents the main point.
  • Provide relevant supporting evidence or examples to justify your claim.
  • Use clear and logical transitions between sentences to maintain coherence.
  • Ensure that each sentence is relevant and adds value to the overall argument.
  • Conclude the paragraph by summarizing the key points or linking the paragraph to the next.
